What Is a Pod?

What Is a Discipline Pod?

A Discipline Pod is a behavioral record captured after a trade. It is minimal, fast to create, and easy to repeat consistently.

Unlike traditional trade journals that focus on price and strategy, Discipline Pods focus entirely on behavior and emotional state.

How It Works

How Pods Work

Each Pod follows a simple lifecycle, from trade to insight.

1

Trade Occurs

A trade closes, whether it is a win, loss, or breakeven. The behavioral response begins immediately.

2

Pod Recorded

Open Promopod. Select result, emotion, rule compliance. Add optional voice note.

3

Data Saved

The Pod is stored as structured behavioral data. Timestamped and categorized.

4

Behavior Analyzed

AI processes the behavioral inputs against your history to detect recurring patterns.

5

Insights Generated

Structured feedback surfaces patterns that would be invisible without consistent tracking.

6

Patterns Detected

Behavioral patterns become measurable, and measurable patterns become controllable.
